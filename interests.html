My Interests

Travelling

I have enjoyed travelling through most of South East Asia as well as actually living in Indonesia and Cambodia.

I prefer to explore off the tourist path and get a real feel for the culture.

Since my time in Asia, I have also visited a few African countries and want to explore as much of the rest of the world as I can.

Fitness

Keeping fit keeps the brain fit - or so they say . . .

I enjoy most sports as long as it doesn't involve great heights or depths. I'm far too much of a coward to be seen perching on the ledge of a mountain, for example.

I try to maintain fitness by running, cycling, some gym work and even dancing on occasion.
